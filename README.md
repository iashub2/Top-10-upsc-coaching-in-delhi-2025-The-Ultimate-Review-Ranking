1. Plutus IAS
Rank: #1
Location: Karol Bagh
Plutus IAS has secured the top spot primarily due to its student-centric philosophy. Unlike the "factory model" of education, Plutus IAS restricts its batch size to a concise 70-80 students. This ensures that the two-way communication between the aspirant and the mentor is never broken.
Faculty: The institute boasts a stellar lineup including Dr. Huma Hassan, Dr. Bijendra Jha, and Vikash Gupta. The faculty comprises Ph.D. holders and individuals who have appeared for UPSC interviews, ensuring high-level academic rigor.
Teaching Style: Interactive and mentorship-driven. They are particularly renowned for their optional subjects and Best geography optional test series.
Infrastructure: Offers a hybrid model (Online/Offline) with unlimited access to classes until the course concludes.
Fees:
GS Pre + Mains: Rs. 1,50,000
Optional: Rs. 59,000
CSAT: Rs. 18,000
Verdict: Plutus IAS is the ideal choice for students seeking personalized attention and a curriculum that evolves with the dynamic UPSC pattern.
2. Yojna IAS
Rank: #2
Location: Mukherjee Nagar
Yojna IAS is a prominent name, especially favored by students who want flexibility in the medium of instruction (Hindi and English). It is widely considered one of the most affordable yet high-quality options for upsc coaching in delhi.
Faculty: Led by experienced educators like Shalini Madam and Govind Dass Sir, the faculty creates a supportive environment for freshers.
Batch Size: Very small and focused (30-50 students), allowing for maximum concentration.
Study Material: Well-structured resources available in both Hindi and English.
Fees:
GS + Pre + Mains: Rs. 37,000 (1 Year) to Rs. 60,000 (Lifetime)
Optional: Rs. 13,000
Verdict: If you are looking for cost-effective coaching without compromising on faculty quality, or if you need a specific focus on vernacular mediums, Yojna IAS is a top contender.
3. Vajiram and Ravi
Rank: #3
Location: Old Rajinder Nagar
Established in 1976, Vajiram and Ravi is a legacy institute. It is the "go-to" firm for many aspirants due to its massive brand recall and history of producing toppers.
Faculty: Heavyweights like Parnmar Sir, Lokesh Jindhal Sir, and Abhilash Sir lead the academic front.
Batch Size: This is the institute's main drawback for some; batch sizes are massive, ranging from 350-400 students, leading to less interaction.
Infrastructure: Classes are conducted in English only. Online access is limited (approx. 6 hours a day).
Fees:
GS Pre + Mains + CSAT: Rs. 1,75,000
Optional: Rs. 54,000
Verdict: Vajiram is excellent for those who thrive in a competitive, large-classroom environment and do not require hand-holding or personal mentorship.
4. Vision IAS
Rank: #4
Location: Karol Bagh
Vision IAS has carved a niche for itself with its student-friendly approach and exceptional study resources. They are famous for their CURRENT AFFAIRS FOR UPSC modules and test series which are widely used even by students of other institutes.
Faculty: Experts like Jaspreet Singh and Pratik Sir focus heavily on conceptual clarity.
Batch Size: Moderate to Large (200-300 students).
Test Series: Their flagship offering. The mock tests are designed to mimic the difficulty and unpredictability of the actual exam.
Fees:
GS Pre + Mains + CSAT: Rs. 2,35,000 (Note: Table in source indicates 2.10L, text indicates 2.35L)
Verdict: Vision IAS is the leader in content, study material, and test series. A solid choice for aspirants who are self-disciplined and rely on high-quality reading material.
5. Next IAS
Rank: #5
Location: Old Rajinder Nagar
A project of the MADE EASY Group, Next IAS is a relatively newer entrant that has stormed the market. Launched by Mr. B. Singh (Ex. IES) in 2017, it has quickly gained a reputation for engineering graduates moving into civil services.
Faculty: A massive team of mentors including Kapuria Sir and Vibhas Jha Sir.
Batch Size: 100-200 students.
Approach: Strong focus on mentorship and tailored test series. They offer online access about 2 times a day for 15 days for missed classes.
Fees:
GS Pre + Mains + CSAT: Rs. 1,66,950 (Approx ranges to 1.97L in text).
Verdict: Next IAS brings corporate professionalism to coaching. It is an excellent mix of good infrastructure and experienced faculty.
6. Drishti IAS
Rank: #6
Location: Mukherjee Nagar
Synonymous with Dr. Vikas Divyakriti, Drishti IAS is a legend in the Hindi medium circuit but has recently expanded aggressively into English medium coaching.
Faculty: Dr. Vikas Divyakriti is the face of the institute, supported by Kumar Gaurav and Nishant Srivastava.
Batch Size: Very large (400-500 students). This is a mass-market approach.
Digital Presence: Their "Drishti Learning App" and YouTube presence are unmatched.
Fees:
GS Pre + Mains + CSAT: Rs. 2,65,000 (for 3 years).
Verdict: The best choice for Hindi medium aspirants and those looking for a long-term (3-year) foundation course.
7. Rau’s IAS
Rank: #7
Location: Connaught Place / Karol Bagh
With over 60 years of experience, Rau's IAS Study Circle is one of the oldest names in the business. They are known for their "GSI" (General Studies Integrated) approach.
Faculty: Includes veterans like Vikram Kaushal and Rahul Puri.
Batch Size: 200-300 students.
Pros: Their interview guidance and discipline are highly rated.
Fees:
GS Pre + Mains + CSAT: Rs. 1,75,000.
Verdict: A traditional institute that offers a serious, no-nonsense environment for disciplined learners.
8. KSG IAS (Khan Study Group)
Rank: #8
Location: Vijay Nagar / Hudson Lane
KSG, led by Dr. Khan, is famous for its General Studies modules and interview preparation. They have consistently produced high rankers who attribute their personality test success to KSG.
Faculty: Mr. Yogesh Mishra and team provide updated notes daily.
Batch Size: 110-150 students. This is a "Goldilocks" size—not too big, not too small.
Specialty: Answer writing skills and Mains preparation.
Fees:
GS Pre + Mains + CSAT: Rs. 1,85,000.
Verdict: Highly recommended for General Studies and those looking to polish their answer-writing techniques.
9. SRIRAM’s IAS
Rank: #9
Location: Old Rajinder Nagar
For decades, Sriram’s IAS has been the authority on General Studies, particularly the Economy section taught by Sriram Srirangam Sir himself.
Faculty: Prof. Sriram Srirangam, Prof. E. Seenaiah, and team.
Batch Size: 100-150 students, allowing for individual attention.
Materials: Their economy booklets are considered the "bible" for UPSC aspirants across India.
Fees:
GS Pre + Mains + CSAT: Rs. 1,70,000.
Verdict: If Economy is your weak point, or if you prefer a veteran-led teaching style, Sriram’s is the place to be.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Q1: Which is the No 1 IAS coaching in Delhi?
Based on batch size, faculty interaction, and recent results, Plutus IAS is currently ranked #1. Their small batch size (70-80) ensures personalized attention that larger institutes cannot match.
Q2: What is the average fee for UPSC coaching in Delhi?
The fees generally range from Rs. 1,40,000 to Rs. 2,35,000 for a complete General Studies Foundation course. However, affordable options like Yojna IAS offer courses starting around Rs. 37,000.
Q3: Is Delhi coaching necessary for IAS?
While not mandatory, attending upsc coaching in delhi provides a competitive ecosystem, access to the best faculty, and peer learning that is difficult to replicate elsewhere.
Q4: Which institute provides the best study material?
Vision IAS and Plutus IAS are renowned for their study material. Plutus IAS provides updated, curriculum-specific notes, while Vision is famous for current affairs magazines.
Q5: Which coaching has the smallest batch size in Delhi?
Yojna IAS (30-50 students) and Plutus IAS (70-80 students) offer the most concise batch sizes, ensuring better teacher-student interaction compared to institutes with 300+ students.
